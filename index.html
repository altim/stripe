Were all about Safety through Design

Spark Free jumper cables are all about safety, design and improvement and we nailed it. With a sleek design and safety first approach we’ve created the worlds first jumper cables with a protective barrier specifically designed to keep you safe.

The Overbite Thats Safer

By adding additional PVC (polyvinyl chloride), raising it on the sides/front of the clamp and reducing the top portion of the copper teeth by a few millimetres, we’ve created an unique “overbite” design that eliminates all contact points.

Global Patent Pending Design

Thanks to our global patent pending design, Spark Free jupmer cables won't spark and ensure safe power transfer. Best part is, Spark Free jumper cables protective barrier limits corrosion leaving you with longer lasting cables.

Where Traditional Jumper Cables Fall Short

Traditional cables have notoriously been known to throw safety out the window. With exposed metal that corrodes easily and exposed teeth comes the increased chance of shock and electrocution. Traditional jumper cables have been in need of a safety and design overhaul since their original inception. Thats where we come in....
